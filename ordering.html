Ordering

The first step is to send your script (it can be sent in parts if you are still working on it) to me at davidguest[at]proofreading.website with any deadlines clearly recorded. After that I will assess your script so that you will know how long I need. This will give you a clear and fixed estimate of the cost of the work.

I will then send a sample of your proofread work for your approval attached to the email containing the offer. Once the offer has been agreed, then the fees should be paid.

The fees will cover also feedback which I consider essential.
